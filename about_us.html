About us

Welcome to Ramkishore Pur Radhanagar High School, a distinguished institution nestled in the heart of South 24 Parganas. Established in the year 1947, our school has embarked on a journey of academic brilliance and holistic development. Our team of 12 dedicated and experienced faculties serves as the cornerstone of our educational philosophy. With a student-teacher ratio of 59:1, we ensure that every student receives personalized attention and guidance, fostering an environment where learning knows no bounds. At Ramkishore Pur Radhanagar High School, we believe in the power of language, with Bengali as the primary medium of instruction. From classes 6 to 12 , our curriculum caters to a wide spectrum of students, nurturing their intellectual growth and curiosity. Our institution thrives with a vibrant student community of approximately 708 individuals, each contributing to the richness of our educational tapestry. Our well-stocked library, boasting a collection of 500 books, provides a gateway to endless knowledge and exploration. Academic excellence is our hallmark, reflected in our exceptional results. A remarkable 100% qualification rate in examinations, coupled with a noteworthy 20% of students achieving the highest grades, underscores our commitment to scholastic achievement. Beyond academics, we embrace the holistic development of our students, nurturing their talents and passions through a variety of extracurricular activities . We envision graduates who are not only academically adept but also well-rounded individuals ready to face the challenges of the modern world. Join us at Ramkishore Pur Radhanagar High School as we continue to shape futures, ignite minds, and create a legacy of educational distinction. Experience a nurturing learning environment where every student is empowered to excel, explore, and evolve.
